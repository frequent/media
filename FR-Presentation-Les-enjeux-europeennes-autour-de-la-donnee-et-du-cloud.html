Quo vadis Europe? - So many crisis, Europe still doesn't evolve? - Do we need a change of perspective in national level for more Europe? - How far does national policy take us in a world of superblocks (eg Aukus)? = Autonomy is hard on small scale. = Autonomy is also a dead end for many crisis eg climate change + covid = We need cooperation - at least on European level. What flavor of democracy and economy? - Russia = no parties, state-influenced markets (oligarchs) - China = 1 party, state controlled markets (government champions) - USA = 2 parties, deregulated markets (global actors) - Europe = multiple parties, reglemented markets (diverse economy) = Diversity and a competitive ecosystem mean a healthy democracy and economy = We need Europe as a referee and normative power (internally and externally) What are European economic challenges in Cloud/Telco and Data markets Resources: (Research shows that diversity results in more competition, better services and better product) Cloud/Telco Questions Risks - Cut submarine cables or internet - 4G/5G core network failure (Telco) - OSS/BSS central panel failure (Cloud) - Destroy DC with accident/graphite/fire - Orchestrated attacks (DDoS etc) or Ransomware (Windows) = Our cloud/telco infrastructure is more vulnerable than we think.
